TED 3

Ted 3 is a 2023 American comedy film directed by Seth MacFarlane and written by MacFarlane, Alec Sulkin, and Wellesley Wild. It is the sequel to Ted 2 (2015) and the third and final installment in the Ted series. The film stars MacFarlane as the title character, along with Mark Wahlberg and Amanda Seyfried. The film was released on June 23, 2023.
